Visit Santa Barbara!

Santa Barbara is one of the most fun and beautiful places you can visit in California. If you want to travel there, you can choose from many different nice hotels. I recommend the Ritz-Carlton Bacara resort. Also, there are four places you should definitely check out.

State Street

State street is a great place to visit if you want to go shopping, have a drink, or get a bite to eat. There are many great stores and bars. There is also Habit Burger, which you have to go to while you're in Santa Barbara.

The Funk Zone

The Funk Zone is a place in Santa Barbara where there is a bunch of bars and wine tasting rooms. This is a fun place to get a drink. Figueroa Mountain Brewing Co. is one of the local brewerys that you will definitely want to go to if you like beer.

The Beach

Be sure to make a trip to the beach! There are some really nice beaches in Santa Barbara. They have Kayak rentals and paddle boards. There are always people playing volleyball. You can also just lay down, relax, and enjoy the great weather that Santa Barbara has to offer!

Nightlife

At night, there are some cool clubs to check out. There are two main clubs called Eos Lounge and Wildcat. Eos plays hip hop music and electronic and usually has two DJ's both inside and outside. Wildcat plays hip hop music and has one DJ but also has an outdoor area with pool tables and a second bar. If you visit Santa Barbara, be ready for a fun time!
